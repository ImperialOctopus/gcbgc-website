About Us

Girton College Board Games Club is a prestigious organisation, created by a dedicated team of like-minded board games enthusiasts over 300 years ago. We are committed to our mission of celebrating and spreading love for board games of all kinds, at all costs.

A revolution in gaming is coming, and we intend to be on the right side of history. Join us.
